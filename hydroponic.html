Made to Suit Your Space

TRADITIONAL FARMING

Yield:

  • Monthly: 5-10 plants (depending on plant type and care)
  • Yearly: 60-120 plants (assuming 5-10 plants/month)

Basic Efforts:

  • Watering: Daily (approx. 1-2 hours)
  • Fertilizing: Weekly (approx. 30 minutes)
  • Pruning: Bi-weekly (approx. 1 hour)

Material:

  • Soil
  • Pots
  • Fertilizers
  • Pest control measures

Power Consumption:

  • None ( manual watering and care)

HYDROPONIC FARMING

Yield:

  • Monthly: 20-30 plants (depending on plant type and care)
  • Yearly: 240-360 plant (assuming 20-30 plants/month)

Basic Efforts:

  • Monitoring: Weekly (approx. 30 minutes)
  • pH adjustments: Bi-weekly (approx. 15 minutes)
  • Nutrient replenishment: Monthly (approx. 1 hour)

Material:

  • Hydroponic system
  • Nutrients
  • pH test kit
  • Water pump (low power consumption)

Power Consumption:

  • Approx. 10-20 watts ( equivalent to a small LED bulb)

NFT Pipes

At Gropzy, we use square NFT pipes that have special holes for better airflow. This design helps your plants get nutrients evenly and has many benefits compared to the round PVC pipes that other companies use.

Enhanced Stability

The square shape is more stable than round PVC pipes, which means they won’t tip over or roll around while you set them up.

Optimal Space Utilization

Let’s us optimise the space vertically and horizontally to arrange plants and get the same amount of nutrients and light, which means you can grow more!

Superior Ventilation

Meticulously designed pipes keeps your plants stay healthy by circulating oxygen.

Efficient Water Flow

The holes are placed just right to help air move around the plant roots.

How we grow?

STAGE 1

•

24-48 hours

Oasis Cube Germination

Seeds are placed in Oasis Cubes, a common medium for hydroponic germination.

The process takes about 24-48 hours, during which the seeds begin to sprout.

STAGE 2

•

Day 1-10

Watering

After germination, the sprouted seedlings are carefully watered for the next 1-10 days.

This stage ensures the roots start developing and absorbing water and nutrients.

STAGE 3

•

24-48 hours

Transplantation

On the 14th day, the seedlings are transplanted from the Oasis Cubes into the main hydroponic system.

At this point, the roots are established enough to thrive in a larger environment.

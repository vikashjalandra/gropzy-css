Our Story

Gropzy was born from a bold vision shared by friends who sought to revolutionize their eating habits with sustainable practices. Dissatisfied with the health impacts of traditional farming, they leveraged their expertise in engineering, science, and agriculture to pioneer a new approach to their own food production.

This led them to hydroponics, a modern method that conserves water and minimizes waste. It began in a small garage as a side project, but now Gropzy is a top company in hydroponics. We keep innovating and working hard to make sustainable food production available to all. Today, we're proud to help a global community of gardeners, farmers, and growers who share our dream of a sustainable future.
